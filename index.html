◈ CORE CONCEPT

▸ THE IDEA

A hyper-playful world where retro game graphics collide with polished 3D cartoon energy. Pixel hearts, coins, power-ups, floating sodas, giant fries, smiling suns, animated palm trees — but rendered premium, not childish.

▸ WHY IT WORKS

Matches Kota Joe's youthful, fun-first energy without losing modern edge. Massive flexibility across stores — each location gets its own unique "level" of the same world.

▸ THE LOOK

Glossy 3D forms with pixel overlays. Think if a roadhouse got turned into the coolest animated game universe in Joburg. Not retro nostalgia alone — cinematic game energy.

▸ THE FEELING

Electric, exciting, impossible to look away from. The more you look, the more you find. Hidden characters, easter eggs, collectible vibes — it rewards attention.

◈ STRATEGIC NOTES
⚠

WATCH OUT — THE RISK

If you lean too pixel-only, it can feel flat and cheap. The fix is making it "pixel meets premium cartoon cinema" — 3D gloss forms with pixel overlays, not flat 8-bit sprites alone. Always think: what would this look like in a Pixar x Street Fighter collab?

★

THE WINNING VERSION

Not retro gaming nostalgia alone. The goal is: "If a roadhouse got turned into the coolest animated game universe in Joburg." Every element rewards a second look. Characters have personality. Products are elevated to hero status. The billboard reads instantly from a car at speed, but pulls you in closer up.
